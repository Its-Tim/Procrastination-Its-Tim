Here are 17 Ways to Help Overcome Procrastination

  1. Eliminate Distractions

    2. Eliminate distractions such as Social media, video games, and television, as these can hinder your productivity.

  2. Understand Procrastination

    3. To understand procrastination, we all have done it before whether we admit it or not. Procrastination is when we delay or postpone a task whether work related or personal.

  3. Employ Time management

    4. One of the most important steps as this is the best way to help you complete tasks on time.

  5. Set Goals

    6. Goals must be achievable or you won't want to try to achieve them because they are difficult. Which will cause you to procrastinate.

    Image by OpenClipart-Vectors from Pixabay

  6. Set deadlines

    7. Deadlines are a good way to help with your time management as it gives you a set time to complete your task.

  7. Break larger tasks into smaller ones

    8. Doing this can lead you to reduced stress and the task isn't as overwhelming.

  8. Reward yourself

    9. Always reward yourself. This can help motivate you to want to complete more tasks. 2 ways to reward yourself are either with a break or maybe social media time.

  9. Practice self-compassion

    10. Treat yourself with kindness, and acceptance, especially when struggling with your tasks, rather than self-criticism.

  10. Establish accountability

    11. Take responsibility for your actions and ensure you follow through on tasks to complete your goals. Also, understand yourself better and develop self-awareness that leads to greater succession in life.

  11. Adjust your environment

    12. Create yourself a spot and setting to which minimizes distractions, making it easier to concentrate and complete tasks.

  12. Change your mindset

    13. Changing your mindset means to view tasks as opportunites for growth and achievements instead of overwhelming.

  13. Ask for help

    14. Asking for help from others isn't a sign of weakness, so don't be afraid to ask for it. It helps to develop better strategies for becoming more productive.

  14. Be kind to yourself

    15. This is basically the same as #8 Practice self-compassion, but this is more towards treating yourself with the same gentleness you would offer a friend facing a similar task.

  15. Prioritize important tasks

    16. Another very important step, as doing lesser tasks or tasks that isn't as important can delay your succession.

    Image by Clker-Free-Vector-Images from Pixabay

  16. Create a routine

    17. Creating a routine will help you to stay on task. An example could be like working for 30 minutes then taking a 5 minute break.

  17. Make a to-do list

    18. Very important as it allows you to see what you need to do and when the task is due.

  18. Be productive

    19. Being productive means to actively engage in tasks, rather than avoid them.
